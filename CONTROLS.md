# Controls

Backrooms Level Zero is designed for Android touch screens in landscape orientation.

## Movement

Use the **left virtual joystick**:

- Push up to walk forward.
- Pull down to walk backward.
- Push left or right to strafe.
- Diagonal movement is supported.

## Look

Swipe on the **right side of the screen** to rotate the camera.

## Sprint

Hold the **SPRINT** button in the lower-right corner to move faster.

## Pause menu

Tap the pause button in the upper-right corner.

The pause menu includes:

- Resume
- Sensitivity decrease/increase
- Graphics quality cycle: Low, Medium, High
- Exit Game

## Objective

The objective appears in the upper-left corner:

```text
Objective: Find the strange exit door
```

Explore the procedural Level 0 layout until you find the unusual door.
